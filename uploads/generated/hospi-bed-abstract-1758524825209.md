This project introduces Hospi-Bed, a pre-booking system for hospital beds designed to mitigate preventable deaths caused by delayed emergency treatment.  The system leverages a robust MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) stack to provide a user-friendly and efficient platform for managing hospital bed availability in real-time.  Hospi-Bed addresses the critical issue of bed scarcity in emergency situations by allowing patients or their representatives to pre-book beds based on predicted need, potentially eliminating lengthy wait times and facilitating timely access to crucial medical care.  The system's features include a centralized database of available beds across various hospitals, a user-friendly interface for both patients and hospital staff, and robust security measures to maintain patient confidentiality.  Through integration with existing hospital systems (future development), Hospi-Bed aims to streamline the emergency response process, reducing response times and improving patient outcomes.  This innovative approach to bed allocation is projected to significantly impact mortality rates associated with delayed emergency treatment, ultimately contributing to a more efficient and responsive healthcare system.  Future work includes exploring integration with ambulance services and developing predictive analytics to further enhance bed allocation efficiency.
