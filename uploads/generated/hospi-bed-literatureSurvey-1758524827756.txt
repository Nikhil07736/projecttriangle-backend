Slide 1: Introducing Hospi-Bed: Revolutionizing Emergency Care

*   Reduces preventable deaths due to delayed hospital bed access.
*   Pre-booking system for hospital beds during emergencies.
*   Leveraging technology for efficient resource allocation.

Slide 2: The Problem: A Critical Shortage

*   Overcrowded hospitals leading to delayed treatment.
*   Lack of real-time bed availability information.
*   Increased mortality rates due to insufficient resources.

Slide 3: Hospi-Bed: The Solution

*   Real-time hospital bed availability tracking.
*   User-friendly pre-booking platform for emergency situations.
*   Seamless integration with hospital management systems.

Slide 4: Technology & Features (MERN Stack)

*   MongoDB database for efficient data management.
*   Express.js backend for robust API development.
*   React frontend for an intuitive user experience.  Node.js server.

Slide 5: Impact & Benefits

*   Faster access to critical care, reducing wait times.
*   Improved hospital resource management and efficiency.
*   Increased patient survival rates and better overall outcomes.
