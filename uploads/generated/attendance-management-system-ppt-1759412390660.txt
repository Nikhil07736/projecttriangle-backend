### **Slide 2: The Hidden Costs of Manual Attendance Tracking**

*   **Significant Administrative Burden:** Hours of valuable staff time are lost each week to manually taking roll, compiling paper records, and re-entering data into spreadsheets or other systems.
*   **Prone to Human Error & Data Inaccuracy:** Manual tracking is susceptible to errors, lost paperwork, and inconsistent records, leading to unreliable data for payroll, compliance, and student/employee monitoring.
*   **Lack of Real-Time Visibility:** Paper-based systems provide no immediate insight into attendance patterns. Management cannot react quickly to absenteeism or tardiness issues as they happen.
*   **Difficult to Scale and Analyze:** As your organization grows, paper-based systems become exponentially more cumbersome and make it nearly impossible to generate meaningful reports or identify long-term trends.
