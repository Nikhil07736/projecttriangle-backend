### **Slide 2: The Hidden Costs of Manual Tracking**

*   **Significant Administrative Overhead:** Manual data entry, paper filing, and report generation consume valuable hours from your staff, diverting focus from core educational or business activities.
*   **Prone to Human Error & Inaccuracy:** Lost sheets, illegible handwriting, and simple data entry mistakes lead to unreliable records, causing payroll disputes, compliance issues, and flawed decision-making.
*   **Lack of Real-Time Visibility:** By the time paper records are compiled, the data is already outdated. You cannot proactively identify absenteeism trends or address issues as they happen.
*   **Security and Compliance Risks:** Paper records are easily lost, damaged, or accessed by unauthorized individuals, posing a significant risk to data privacy and regulatory compliance.

*   **Eliminates Paper, Reduces Errors:** By digitizing the entire workflow from marking to reporting, we eliminate manual data entry, drastically improving the accuracy and integrity of your attendance records.
